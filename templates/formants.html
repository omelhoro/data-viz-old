This data shows the formants measured in task 7 (reading a story).This data is a spin-off of my rhythm-metrics. The annotation is as follows: The plot shows SAMPA-symbols as the ideal pronunciation. So ideally a word like "стена" is pronounced "stin'a", but if a child pronounced it like "stena" then the ideal "i" is in the region of "e".

A ideal picture of formants should look like a triangle, but here only one participant shows that; the others have a rather unstable pronunciation, so there is less vowel reduction than expected.
