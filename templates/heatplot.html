This plot shows the results of task 3 in our project. The children were asked to name the pictures shown to them. The pictures were chosen accordingly to some phonological thoughts, but they also had a frequency component. In the plot biographical data is correlated against frequency of words (source: Sharov and Ljashevskaja).

Interestingly there is a correlation between phonetic measurements and the spoken frequency of the words, but to smaller extent with the biography of the children.
